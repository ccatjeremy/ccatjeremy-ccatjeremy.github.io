About Muscle Lamp
The lamp holder with muscle texture extends in six hands grabbing the halo, like lamp itself swallows the light. All men naturally desire to gain better position and reputation, this lamp has the same goal.
Background
It's the first time I used 3D printer to built a prototype, I was even not familiar with 3D software at that time. It's a challenge for me to reach some new process to make a model. The lamp holder is made of ABS, and the halo is made of acrylic, there are some LED strips in that.
