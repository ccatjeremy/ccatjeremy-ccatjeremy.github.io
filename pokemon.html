Product Design
Pokemon Lamp Design Series
Weekly Project, 2017
Victreebel / week 7
Victreebel is featured on the bell-shaped body and the long, brown vine growing out of it. The lamp arm is inspired by the long vine, and the lamp shade hanging from the ceiling looks just like victreebel hanging there luring its victims.
Magnemite / week 6
The viewers can only see the big eye and slight part of the horseshoe magnets because of the black lamp shade. This lamp hanged from the ceiling makes you feel the magnemites floating there looking at you in the dark.
Alakazam / week 5
The lamp shade is inspired by its face and the additional spike protrude from each cheek. I imitated its body and added some brown armor-like silicon parts to the rotating shafts and the lamp holders. The screws on the rotating shafts are designed looked like spoon.
Wailord / week 4
This fluorescent lamp is suitable for office and school. It's looked like a huge wailord floating above you. The reflexed tail is designed as a support to be fixed the lamp to the ceiling.
Gloom / week 3
The lamp shade is inspired by the reddish-brown flower on gloom's head, and the idea of lamp arm gained from its orange leaves.
Zapdos / week 2
Zapdos features in its yellow spiky feathers and the black feathers covering the back of its wings and inner tail feathers. The lamp shade is in a spiky shape. Pushing the black feather could switch the lamp.
Kingler / week 1
The idea of lamp shade is from kingler's main feature, its red claws. I used the polygon style to imitate the roughly shell. The lamp arm is inspired by its claw-like long, thin legs. And the lamp holder surrounding by light tan shell make itself look like kingler's lower-half.
