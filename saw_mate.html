About Saw Mate
Facing the aging industry, we’re not only dedicated to reduce the heavy burdens the carpenters carrying during the works and enhance their efficiency of working, but also trying to offer them a better user experience by designing the Saw Mate.
Background
It is a special habit and custom of Taiwanese decoration carpentry to carry the wood and the tools they may use straight to the interior decoration sites, and then follow by measuring, cutting and assembling. The conditions of the environment and the tools at the sites are much worse than the factories, so many problems of the safety have aroused.
Concept
Saw Mate brings a new era for the interior carpentry industry by redesigning the carpenter table which is the heaviest and hardest to carry. Facing the aging industry, we’re not only dedicated to reduce the heavy burdens the carpenters carrying during the works and enhance their efficiency of working, but also trying to offer them a better user experience by designing the Saw Mate.
Feature
Carrying:
-By designing in an ergonomic way, the handles can make the carrying progress more effort-saving.

Lifting Guide:
-The different materials can guide the users to lift the boards in an easier way.
Transporting:
-Boards designed in the same size are better for transportation.

Measuring Scale System:
-We designed a new appearence of the measuring scale system which let users easily read the number. And also the numbers will not be covered by the auxiliary bar .
Corresponding Color System:
-We paint different color on each sides to make the users set Saw Mate ready without hesitation.

Red Alert Zone:
-We painted the area around the saw in red to alarm the user when they are cutting small peices of wood.
Better Assembly Steps
Step 1:
Open both two sides of bottom-boards and lock the locks.

Step 2:
Close the left upper-board along the narrow cut by holding the handles of different material.
Step 3:
Set the lifter which is connected with the left upper-board.handles of different material.

Step 4:
Let the saw machine slide along the rail under the left upper-board.
Step 5:
Close the right upper-board and lock the connecting lock with the left half.

Step 6:
Set ready the auxiliary bar and the pushing board.
