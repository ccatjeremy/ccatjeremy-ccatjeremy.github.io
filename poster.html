Traffic Safety Poster Design
Taoyuan Traffic Police Corps , 2017
About the posters
In my alternative military service life, I worked in the Taoyuan traffic police corps as a graphic designer. The main goal was to declare traffic safety and design some prints, and the posters was one of my works. ‘Super Easy’ is the theme of the poster, which means all the actions about traffic safety are pieces of cake. I designed all of the patterns in the corner for keeping the poster looked legible and clear.
