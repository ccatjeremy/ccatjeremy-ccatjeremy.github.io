About Zipco
“Zip” means zipper, and “co” means combine, we hope everyone could create their own bag!
Zip your own bag!
We use zippers to bring different bags together. The bags with different functions are flexible to different situation. You can create what you want by the various bags we provided.
Big bag:
Drawstring and Backpack, there are three positions to zip on the backpack, and two on the drawstring.
Small bag with a hole:
The small hole is suitable for power bank and cable. You can take the small bag away instead of the whole backpack when you go out causally.

Pouch:
It’s suitable for water bottle or pens. It could be detached as a pencil case.
Small bag with card cases:
The card case is designed for putting the card you need in everyday life.

Middle bag:
A long bag with two positions to zip. It’s suitable for bigger common items.
Decorative items:
They are not really useful, just for beauty and fun.
